OUR STORY


Cooking is an act of love, not commerce.


Chef Jean-Pierre Laurent trained under legendary figures in Lyon and Paris before opening Maison Dorée in 2008. His philosophy is simple: extraordinary ingredients treated with extraordinary respect.


Every morning begins at the markets of Île-de-France. Every menu is dictated by what arrived that day — never by habit, never by convenience.
