OUR STORY


About Fresh Bites

"The market tells me what to cook. The season tells me how.

My guests remind me why."


— Jean-Pierre Laurent

A Restaurant Born from Obsession


Jean-Pierre Laurent spent his formative years in the kitchens of Lyon, Bordeaux, and London before returning to Paris to open Maison Dorée in a quiet rue near the Madeleine. He had one obsession: to cook with perfect ingredients, handled with perfect technique, served without pretension.


The restaurant began with 28 covers. Today it seats 42, with a private dining room for 12. The first Michelin star came in 2010. The second in 2016. The philosophy has not changed.


Every week, Chef Laurent visits the Marché d'Aligre personally. Every day, the menu is rewritten. Nothing is preserved from yesterday unless yesterday was extraordinary.


WHAT WE BELIEVE


Our Values

O1


Seasonality


We write our menu at the market each morning. No ingredient travels more than 200km to reach our kitchen.

O2


Tradition


Classical French technique is our foundation. We honour it with discipline — and occasionally, with joy, defy it.

O3


Humanity


We are a team of 22 people. Every guest is welcomed as if entering a private home. That is not a policy; it is who we are.

O4


Sustainability


Zero food waste since 2021. Our composting programme feeds the gardens of three local farms that supply us.

THE PEOPLE

Meet the Team

HEAD CHEF & FOUNDER

Jean-Pierre Laurent

Trained at Le Gavroche under Michel Roux Jr., then under Alain Passard at L'Arpège. JP opened Maison Dorée in 2008 with a singular vision: pure, seasonal French cuisine.

PASTRY CHEF

Sophie Marchand

A graduate of École Ferrandi, Sophie's desserts have been described as 'sculptures you can eat'. Her tarte Tatin and soufflé au chocolat are the things of legend.

SOMMELIER

Marcus Bellini

WSET Level 4 certified, Marcus curates one of Paris's most celebrated wine lists — 600 labels spanning old-world classics to natural wines from emerging producers.

MAITRE D'HOTEL

Chloé Bertrand

With 14 years in front-of-house at three-star establishments across Europe, Chloé orchestrates the dining room with quiet precision and genuine warmth.
